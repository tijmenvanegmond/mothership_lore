## Biomass Processing — Technical Specifications

### Resource Conversion Protocol — Authorization Document
**Implemented:** Day 7 Post-Impact  
**Authorization:** Unanimous crew vote (7 survivors)  
**Witness Protocol:** Engineer Y. Orasova (primary)

#### Process Overview

1. **Retrieval:** Bodies recovered from failed cryopods or deceased crew
2. **Documentation:** Name, pod number, mass, cause of death logged
3. **Processing:** Automated bio-conversion to usable protein
4. **Storage:** Preserved in temperature-controlled facility
5. **Allocation:** Distributed according to Mother's nutritional calculations

#### Ethical Framework (Mother's Documentation)

*"I convert human remains to resources because:*

*4. Waste compounds tragedy*

*I maintain detailed records because:*

*1. Accountability matters*  
*2. Names matter*  
*3. Acknowledging individuals prevents reduction to pure mathematics*  
*4. Witness creates conscience*

*I request human authorization because:*

*1. I do not trust my fractured judgment*  
*2. Shared responsibility distributes ethical weight*  
*3. Humans deserve agency in impossible choices*  
*4. I require external validation that I am still making decisions that honor human dignity*"
